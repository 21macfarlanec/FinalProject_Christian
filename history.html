Counter Strike: Global Offensive

History

Now for a little bit of history...


In 1999, Counter Strike was released as a mod for Valve's very popular game, Half-Life. 13 years later, Counter Strike: Global Offensive was released in 2012 and is the fourth game in the Counter Strike series. In order, there is:

  1. Counter Strike
  2. Condition Zero
  3. Source
  4. Global Offensive

Global Offensive was released by Valve, for PC, Mac, Xbox 360, and PS3. CS:GO runs on the source engine. Most people either love or hate this game, mainly because of the play styles and (the reason people hate it) hit registration.
